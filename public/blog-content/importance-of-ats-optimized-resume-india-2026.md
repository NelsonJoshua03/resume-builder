About the job
About the Role

We are looking for a skilled Design Engineer to join our team and contribute to medical device product design and development. This role involves design control, risk management, and documentation compliance to meet global regulatory standards.


Key Responsibilities

Design and develop mechanical components for medical devices
Ensure compliance with medical device design control and regulatory requirements
Manage design changes, documentation, and risk analysis
Perform DFMEA and support DHF/DMR remediation
Collaborate with cross-functional teams for product lifecycle management
Utilize PLM tools like Windchill for design documentation
Prepare detailed CAD models and drawings using SolidWorks
Apply GD&T principles for accurate design specifications


Required Skills & Qualifications

Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Mechanical Engineering
Hands-on experience in medical device design control
Proficiency in SolidWorks, GD&T, and PLM tools (Windchill or equivalent)
Strong knowledge of risk management and DFMEA
Excellent written and verbal communication skills
Preferred: Experience in medical device industry
