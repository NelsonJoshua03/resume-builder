"Assisted in maintenance of 50+ industrial machines with 99.5% uptime"

4. Achievements Beyond Academics
SAE competitions

Paper publications

Workshop certifications

Industry-Specific Resume Adjustments
For Automotive Roles: Highlight engine testing, vehicle dynamics, emission control projects
For Manufacturing: Focus on quality control, process optimization, lean manufacturing
For Oil & Gas: Emphasize piping design, pressure vessel calculations, safety protocols
For Design Engineering: Showcase 3D modeling, simulation results, prototype development

Common Mechanical Engineering Resume Mistakes to Avoid
Too many tools listed: Focus on proficiency levels (Beginner/Intermediate/Expert)

Generic objective statement: Replace with "Aspiring Design Engineer seeking..."

Missing ATS keywords: Include terms like "GD&T," "Tolerance Stack-up," "FMEA"

No quantifiable results: Always include percentages, time saved, costs reduced

Ignoring soft skills: Problem-solving, teamwork, and communication matter
