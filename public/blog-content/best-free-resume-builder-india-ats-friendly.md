## What Is a Resume Builder?

A **resume builder** is an online tool that helps you create a professional resume using pre-designed templates. Instead of worrying about formatting, fonts, or alignment, you simply:

- Enter your details  
- Choose a resume template  
- Download your resume as a PDF  

Modern resume builders are designed to be **ATS-friendly**, meaning they work well with recruitment software used by companies like TCS, Infosys, Accenture, Wipro, Amazon, and startups.

---

## Why ATS-Friendly Resume Matters in India?

An **ATS (Applicant Tracking System)** scans resumes for:

- Keywords from the job description  
- Proper headings (Experience, Skills, Education)  
- Clean formatting without tables or images  

❌ Fancy designs  
❌ Icons and graphics  
❌ Multiple columns  

These often cause **instant rejection**.

An ATS-friendly resume builder ensures:
- Single-column layout  
- Standard fonts  
- Correct keyword placement  
- Recruiter-readable formatting

## How to Create an ATS-Friendly Resume Online (Step-by-Step)

### Step 1: Choose the Right Resume Template
Select a **simple, professional resume template** optimized for ATS.

### Step 2: Add Job-Specific Keywords
Use keywords from the job description:
- Skills
- Tools
- Job titles
- Certifications

### Step 3: Write Impactful Bullet Points
Use action words:
- Developed
- Managed
- Optimized
- Executed

Example:
> Managed project documentation for 4 pipeline projects across Texas using PCMS software.

### Step 4: Download as PDF
Always download your resume in **PDF format** unless the job asks otherwise.

## Resume Builder for Freshers vs Experienced Professionals

### Resume Builder for Freshers
Focus on:
- Education
- Internships
- Projects
- Skills
- Certifications

### Resume Builder for Experienced Candidates
Focus on:
- Achievements
- Metrics (%, ₹, numbers)
- Tools & software
- Industry keywords

A good resume builder adapts to **both profiles**.
