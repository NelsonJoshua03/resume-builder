## Government Job Resume Format for India

Specialized format for UPSC, SSC, Banking and other government exams.

## Key Sections for Government Resumes

- **Educational Qualifications:** Detailed marks and percentages
- **Work Experience:** Government or relevant public sector experience
- **Training/Certifications:** Any government-recognized certifications
- **Languages:** Proficiency in Hindi and English
- **Computer Skills:** Basic computer certifications

## Formatting Tips

- Use simple, clean format without graphics
- Include all personal details as required
- Follow the specific format mentioned in the job notification
