## ğŸ¯ Why ATS-Optimized Resume is CRITICAL for TCS Walk-ins

Before we dive into the preparation, here's a crucial fact: **Over 90% of resumes in India get rejected by Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS)** before they even reach human recruiters.

### The TCS Resume Screening Process:
1. **Initial ATS Scan:** Your resume is first scanned by automated systems
2. **Keyword Matching:** Systems look for specific skills and experience
3. **Format Compatibility:** Complex formats often get rejected
4. **Human Review:** Only ATS-passing resumes reach recruiters

TCS, being India's largest IT employer, receives thousands of applications for each walk-in. Without an ATS-friendly resume, your application might get rejected even before you reach the interview desk.

## ğŸ› ï¸ How to Create an ATS-Friendly Resume for TCS in 5 Minutes

### Step 1: Use the Right Format
- **Avoid:** Tables, columns, graphics, headers/footers
- **Use:** Simple, clean single-column format
- **Fonts:** Standard fonts (Arial, Calibri, Times New Roman)

### Step 2: Include Keywords from Job Description
TCS's job descriptions contain specific keywords. Make sure these appear in your resume:
- For QA Role: "Playwright", "automation testing", "CI/CD"
- For .NET Role: "ASP.NET", "Angular", "REST API", "MS SQL"
- For SAP Roles: "SAP SD", "SAP FICA", "configuration", "implementation"

### Step 3: Structure Your Resume Properly
1. **Contact Information** (Name, Phone, Email, Location)
2. **Professional Summary** (2-3 lines highlighting key skills)
3. **Technical Skills** (Grouped by category)
4. **Work Experience** (Reverse chronological order with achievements)
5. **Education** (Simple list with percentages)
6. **Certifications** (If applicable)
