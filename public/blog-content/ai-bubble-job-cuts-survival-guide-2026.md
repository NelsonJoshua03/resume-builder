The Story of the AI Bubble
Act 1: The Hype Train (2023-2025)
Companies scrambled to hire AI specialists
Salaries skyrocketed with no proven ROI
"AI-powered" became the magic phrase for funding

Act 2: Reality Check (Late 2025)
Companies realized AI implementation costs
Most AI projects showed minimal business impact
Investors demanded tangible results

Act 3: The Great Correction (2026)
Mass layoffs in bloated AI departments
Consolidation of AI teams
Focus shifted to profitable AI applications only
