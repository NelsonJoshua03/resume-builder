## Common Resume Mistakes Indians Make (And How to Fix Them)

### Mistake 1: **Wrong File Format**
**Problem:** Sending .docx files that get corrupted
**Solution:** Always export as PDF

### Mistake 2: **Too Long or Too Short**
**Problem:** 4-page resumes or half-page resumes
**Solution:** Keep it 1-2 pages maximum

### Mistake 3: **No Contact Information**
**Problem:** Missing email or wrong phone format
**Solution:** Include email, phone (+91 format), and LinkedIn

### Mistake 4: **Spelling and Grammar Errors**
**Problem:** Typos that create bad impression
**Solution:** Use spell check and proofread carefully

### Mistake 5: **No Quantifiable Achievements**
**Problem:** Just listing responsibilities
**Solution:** Add numbers and results (e.g., "Increased sales by 25%")
