## Why 90% of Resumes Get Rejected by ATS Systems in India

### Understanding the ATS Black Box

Most job seekers in India face a harsh reality: their carefully crafted resumes never reach human eyes. With over **90% of resumes getting rejected by ATS systems**, understanding this automated gatekeeper is your first step toward interview success.

**Common ATS Rejection Reasons:**
- Complex formatting (tables, columns, headers/footers)
- Missing industry-specific keywords
- Unreadable fonts or graphics
- Incorrect file formats
- Lack of quantifiable achievements

### The Indian ATS Landscape

Indian companies like **TCS, Infosys, Wipro, and Amazon** use sophisticated ATS systems that filter based on:
1. **Keyword matching** - JD-specific terms
2. **Experience validation** - Years and relevance
3. **Education criteria** - Degree and institutions
4. **Location preferences** - Tier 1 vs Tier 2 cities
5. **Skill alignment** - Must-have vs nice-to-have skills

## Top 15 Resume FAQs Every Indian Job Seeker Asks

### FAQ 1: How long should my resume be for Indian companies?

**Answer:** For experienced professionals (5+ years): 2 pages maximum. For freshers: 1 page. Indian recruiters typically spend **6-8 seconds** scanning a resume.

**Pro Tip:** Use CareerCraft's template that automatically optimizes content length for your experience level.

### FAQ 2: Should I include a photo on my Indian resume?

**Answer:** **Generally NO**, unless specifically applying for modeling, acting, or certain international roles where it's customary. Indian corporate roles typically prefer no photo.

### FAQ 3: What's the best resume format for ATS?

**Answer:** Reverse chronological format with clear headings, bullet points, and **standard fonts** (Arial, Calibri, Times New Roman). CareerCraft's **ATS-optimized template** handles this automatically.

### FAQ 4: How many skills should I list?

**Answer:** 10-15 relevant skills categorized as:
- **Technical Skills** (Programming, Tools)
- **Soft Skills** (Leadership, Communication)
- **Domain Skills** (Industry-specific)

### FAQ 5: Should I customize my resume for each application?

**Answer:** **ABSOLUTELY YES!** Each application should have:
1. **JD keywords** integrated naturally
2. **Relevant achievements** highlighted
3. **Company-specific terminology** included

**Quick Customization Tip:** Use CareerCraft's "Quick Edit" feature to create multiple versions in minutes.

### FAQ 6: How do I handle employment gaps?

**Answer:** Be honest but strategic:
- **Upskilling periods** → Frame as professional development
- **Personal time** → Mention valuable skills gained
- **Job search time** → Show networking/learning activities

### FAQ 7: What achievements should I include?

**Answer:** Follow the **CAR framework**:
- **C**hallenge (What was the problem?)
- **A**ction (What did you do?)
- **R**esult (Quantify the outcome)

**Example:** "Increased sales by 35% by implementing a new CRM system, resulting in ₹50L additional revenue."

### FAQ 8: How important is the resume summary?

**Answer:** **CRITICAL** - It's your 30-second elevator pitch. Include:
- Years of experience
- Core expertise
- Key achievements
- Career objective

**CareerCraft Feature:** Auto-generates powerful summaries based on your profile.

### FAQ 9: Should I include references?

**Answer:** Simply state "References available upon request." Don't waste valuable space with contact details.

### FAQ 10: What file format is best for ATS?

**Answer:** **PDF** for human reading, but ensure it's ATS-friendly. CareerCraft generates **ATS-parsable PDFs** that maintain formatting across all devices.

### FAQ 11: How to handle multiple short-term jobs?

**Answer:** Group relevant short-term roles under "Consulting Experience" or "Project-based Work" with total duration and key projects.

### FAQ 12: Are hobbies and interests necessary?

**Answer:** Include only if they demonstrate **relevant skills** (Team sports = teamwork, Chess = strategic thinking) or are specifically requested.

### FAQ 13: How to format contact information?

**Answer:** Top of page with:
- Professional email (not funny nicknames)
- Indian mobile number with country code (+91)
- LinkedIn profile URL (optimized)
- City/State (No need for full address)

### FAQ 14: What's the ideal font size?

**Answer:** 10-12 points for body text, 14-16 for headings. CareerCraft templates use **optimal readability sizing**.

### FAQ 15: How often should I update my resume?

**Answer:** **Every 3-6 months** or immediately after:
- Completing a major project
- Learning new skills
- Receiving awards/recognition
- Changing responsibilities

## International Resume Guide for Indian Professionals 2025

### Global Resume Standards vs Indian Formats

**Key Differences to Note:**

| Aspect | Indian Resume | International Resume |
|--------|--------------|---------------------|
| **Length** | 1-2 pages | 1 page (US), 2 pages (EU) |
| **Personal Details** | Often includes DOB, marital status | Never includes these |
| **Photo** | Sometimes included | Rarely included |
| **Format** | Reverse chronological | Reverse chronological |
| **References** | Sometimes included | "Available upon request" |

### Country-Specific Requirements

**United States:**
- No personal information
- Action verbs at sentence start
- Quantifiable achievements emphasized

**United Kingdom:**
- 2 pages maximum
- Personal statement at top
- Clear career progression

**Australia:**
- 3-4 pages acceptable
- Detailed referees section
- Specific visa status if applicable

**Middle East:**
- Photo often required
- Personal details expected
- Conservative formatting preferred

### Visa-Sponsorship Statement

**Crucial addition for international applications:**
> "Authorized to work in [Country] / Requiring [Type] visa sponsorship"
